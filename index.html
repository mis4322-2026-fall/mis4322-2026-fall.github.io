What this course is about

MIS 4322 is designed to help you think clearly about how organizations use information systems to compete, collaborate, and make decisions. The best way to succeed is to connect the concepts to what you already see in daily life: apps, platforms, automation, analytics, and digital workflows.

  • Learn the language of business technology without losing sight of the business problem.
  • Practice making structured decisions with data, process, and user needs in mind.
  • Build confidence discussing current technology trends in a professional setting.

Course expectations

Bring curiosity, consistency, and a willingness to participate. Most success in a course like this comes from staying current and asking good questions early.

  • Check the course site regularly for updates and deadlines.
  • Complete work on time and communicate early when something comes up.
  • Engage with your classmates respectfully and professionally.

Weekly rhythm

A steady routine helps keep the course manageable and reduces last-minute stress.

Before class

Review the assigned material and note any terms or ideas that feel unfamiliar.

During class

Focus on the examples, ask questions, and connect the lesson to a real organization or app.

After class

Complete the assigned practice or reflection while the material is still fresh.
